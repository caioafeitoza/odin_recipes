Brazilian Chicken Strogonoff

The Brazilian Chicken Strogonoff is an adaption of the classic russian dish. It's very popular and loved recipe among many brazilians.

Ingredients

  • 500g of breast chicken
  • 200g of cream
  • 250ml of tomato sauce
  • 2 spoons of mustard
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken in small cubes;
  2. Grilled with olive oil, salt and pepper;
  3. When the cubes get golden, pour the tomato sauce and mustard;
  4. Let the sauce get consistency;
  5. Turn off the fire and put the cream.
